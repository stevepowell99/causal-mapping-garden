**Outcome description**: During 2024 and 2025, thirty <redacted> plaintiffs testified in court for the Lundin war crimes trial in Sweden. 
**Significance of the outcome**: This type of trial is very rare. It is important for truth-finding and justice that people from South Sudan, the victims and rightholders, are able to testify about what happened to them. The responsible <redacted> authorities are not capable of enabling plaintiffs to travel to Sweden and present themselves in court. PAX taking over this rule was therefor crucial to enable rightholders to speak in this judicial procedure. Thanks to PAX's presence and network in South Sudan, we could ensure communication, security, internal travel, visa applications, vaccinations, international travel arrangements, accompaniemant, translation services and other necessary services.  
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: International 
**Contribution of the programme**: PAX, together with our partner Global Idé, implemented the high risk and complicated effort, that depended on close cooperation between 9 people in five countries. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Global Idé ensured communication and cooperation, PAX Juba Office ensured local implementation and security.
