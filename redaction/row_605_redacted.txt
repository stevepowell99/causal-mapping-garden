**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, in <LOC> members of the South Sudan Youth Constitution Working Group increased from 35 members to 50 members
**Significance of the outcome**: This is significant because at the beginning of the program the youth didn’t have a lot of information about the R-ARCSS and the constitution making process. They also did not know how they can participate and shape the process. The increasing in number indicates the increased awareness,  interest and involvement in lobbying for the constitution making process
**Additional information (use if needed) **:  The youth had limited knowledge about the constitution making process, had no idea of how and when they can be involved in the process. The South Sudan Constitution working group through their sensitization events was able to explain that  and draw more youth to join the lobby group
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: Organisation
**Contribution of the programme**: The program provided the youth with mentorship and coaching by training them in leadership, advocacy and public speaking. The program also provided the members with seed grants to finance theirr awareness raising initiatives on the constitution making process
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: UNMISS and UNDP also lobby for the enactment of the R-ARCSS and constitution making process
