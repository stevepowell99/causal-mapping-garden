**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, young women and girls in <LOC>, Lebanon, formed the first women-volleyball team, in a context where such opportunities were previously limited and restricted and in conserved communities.
**Significance of the outcome**: Previously there were no safe spaces to give expression to women and girls in <LOC>. Peace of Arts conducted awareness sessions with families and leaders to promote the importance of girls’ participation in sports and public life. We also organized confidence-building workshops for young women and girls and provided training in leadership and teamwork. Additionally, we supported the formation of the volleyball team by providing equipment, facilitating access to practice spaces, and connecting the team with experienced coaches. Continuous mentorship and community events helped sustain interest and visibility for the initiative.
