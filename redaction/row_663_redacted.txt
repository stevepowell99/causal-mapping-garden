**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, a male HRD in Ethiopia launched an initiative called Wendata to challenge harmful gender norms through social media, a space dominated by women and girl-led efforts. This followed a training in militarised masculinity.  
**Significance of the outcome**: Traditionally, advocacy for women and girls' rights in Ethiopia are women-led effort. However, following a training in militarised masculinity, a male HRD was able to establish a space that has enable men to engage in similar advocacy for women and girls rights, focusing on eliminating gender norms on social media.  It institutionalizes a model where men lead efforts to advocate for women’s rights is appreciated as an approach to tackle the negative gender norms. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The Male HRD developed a deep understanding of the linkages between gender, masculinity, patriarchy, and feminist peace after completing training in a pilot project on militarized masculinities.
