**Outcome description**: During a meeting held in <redacted>, in the city of Kisangani, the <redacted> nature conservation state institute (ICCN) and civil society organizations appointed the SCC partner organization CDJP-Kisangani to hold the secretariat of a new grievance mechanism for the Lomami National Park in Tshopo province.
**Significance of the outcome**: PAX and others had long called for a grievance mechanism for the Lomami National Park to address grievances of the local communities towards the park management and authorities, regarding social tensions and human rights abuses. The fact that CDJP-Kisangani was appointed to hold the secretariat gives credibility and a certain degree of independence to this mechanism.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: CDJP-Kisangani has been PAX's main partner for our work on mediation between the park management and local communities.
