**Outcome description**: In his report on the human rights situation in Burundi of 11 August 2023, the United Nations Special Rapporteur on Burundi specifically mentioned the phenomenon of enforced disappearances in <redacted>, referring to FOCODE.
**Significance of the outcome**: The authorities in Burundi, including the President of the Republic, have long denied the existence of enforced disappearances and have never taken steps to investigate these cases. The UN Special Rapporteur recognises and identifies serious human rights violations, but he has not yet mentioned enforced disappearances as clearly, nor FOCODE's contribution. The clear mention of these facts is important for international advocacy, to convince international actors that Burundi does not respect human rights and that it must be held accountable for these serious violations.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The reference to <person> by the United Nations Special Rapporteur reinforces the credibility of FOCODE's documentation work on the phenomenon of enforced disappearances.
