**Outcome description**: During <redacted>, for the first time, Mr. "A", a participant in Program ABB (a program developed to prevent violence by promoting positive masculinities in fatherhood) in the <LOC>, proudly shared with the group and the facilitator during the last session, that he was helping his spouse in performing domestic tasks and in taking care of their children, applying new parenting techniques and communication skills in their interactions with their children.
**Significance of the outcome**: Before the intervention, Mr. "A" had never participated in household chores nor applied positive parenting techniques, preferring instead to conform to traditional gender roles. This shift is significant because Mr. "A" is directly challenging unequal gender norms in a conservative patriarchal community, contributing to the project's goal of advocating for gender equality. This shift holds immense significance for the project's overarching objective of advocating for gender equality. By defying traditional expectations and embracing progressive practices, Mr. 'A' becomes a catalyst for change within his community. His actions serve as tangible evidence of the project's impact, demonstrating the feasibility and effectiveness of challenging outdated gender stereotypes.
