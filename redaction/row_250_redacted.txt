**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, in Zemio, Central African Republic, a high-ranking LRA commander signed a declaration expressing the wish of dozens of LRA fighters to lay down their arms and be repatriated to Uganda.
**Significance of the outcome**: This commitment was a key element in the effective demobilization of the remaining LRA groups in CAR. It subsequently enabled PAX and APRu to lobby the <redacted> government to get the DDR process underway.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The demobilization of the LRA was a major issue. Firstly, it offered better prospects for the combatants and their families, who were living in very precarious conditions in the CAR. Secondly, the demobilization of the LRA marked the end of this armed group's presence in the area. The LRA had committed enormous atrocities against civilians, and its presence was a permanent threat.
