**Outcome description**: The Ministry of Interior via the Inspector General of the Police ordered the release of <redacted> Irakoze, a street fruit vendor in downtown <LOC>, in <redacted>, who was violently attacked by police officers. The two police officers were arrested.
**Significance of the outcome**: The release of the mother of the street vendor and the arrest of the police officers shows that the government is susceptible to public pressure, which followed after the partner organisation published a video of the mistreatment of the woman. The video shocked thousands of persons and gave a bad image to the police, which is why they took quick action. Like other releases, this release had an impact on the whole population so that they would continue to denounce such cases and put pressure on local authorities, as that could reach farther than they would think.
