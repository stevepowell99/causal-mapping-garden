**Social actor**: Engaged citizens

**Contribution of the programme**: After recruiting 20 activists from the community, the young participants participated in 16 sessions of the Citizen Actor Program by SADA, where they explored topics related to masculinity, violence, discrimination and social bias, emotions, boundaries, safe spaces, our relationship with our bodies, and trauma, using Playback Theatre methodology. In addition, they attended two SADA workshops focused on conflict sensitivity, context analysis, lesson plan design, and facilitation skills. As a result, they were able to design and deliver five different community performances to their communities.
