**Outcome description**: In Mali, thanks to the skills developed during the drafting of the MDR report on 2023, in <redacted> the NGO ADD drew up a thematic report on the situation of conflict-related sexual violence in Liptako-Gourma to inform national and international opinion of the situation
**Significance of the outcome**: This report provides ADD with a degree of expertise in drafting thematic reports based on monitoring data. This report is important in the sense that it gives some recognition to ADD's expertise in the field of human rights but especially in the fight against violence against women, a field in which the organisation has references. The report was shared on the NGO's website, while its partner SAGOPS also shared the report with its national and international network.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: As part of the implementation of the conflict-related sexual violence project, it was planned to produce a report on conflict-related sexual violence. ADD used the data collected to draw up the report
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**:  This report is an important exercise that will enable ADD to develop its skills in writing thematic reports for the MDR.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: The NGO Sahéliennes pour la Justice, la Gouvernance, la Paix et la Sécurité (SAGOPS), a partner of ADD, made a major contribution to the production of the report by providing a gender expert. 
**Rating contribution**: little
