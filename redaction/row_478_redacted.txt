**Outcome description**: At the 2024 UNGA First Committee, <LOC> demonstrated increasing recognition of the need to address pressing issues around armed drones through statements and formation of the Core Group of States on UAVs.
**Significance of the outcome**: For a long time, armed drones have been absent from the UN disarmament debate, untill Portugal's announcement of intention to champion this issue in 2023. As a follow-up to that announcement, Portugal initiated several joint initiatives around armed drones at the UN, including a Joint Declaration in <redacted>, and formation of the Core Group of States on UAVs at the UNGA First Committee, which presented ideas for the next steps in the joint statement delivered in <redacted>. In parallel, other <LOC> also drew more attention to this issue in their national interventions, in striking contrast to <redacted> when drones were rarely mentioned. Yet, there are still no concrete policy initiatives to address concerns around UAV proliferation.
