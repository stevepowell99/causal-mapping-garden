**Outcome description**: In 2021, following the virtual scenario building workshop conducted by DefendDefenders, local partner CSOs in Ethiopia were able to conduct voter education and election observation without harassments and interference of the state.
**Significance of the outcome**: Without the scenario building workshop Civil Society Organisations were not able to effectively particiapate in election observation. Additionally, Civil Society Organisations didn’t have an election manual to guide election observation and voter education.
