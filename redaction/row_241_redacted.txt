**Outcome description**: In 2023, following the documentation of a case of rape in the <LOC> cercle, the victim's family sought the assistance of the MDR project monitor, after which a complaint was drawn up with the support of the TRIJEUD legal clinic in <LOC> and the case is currently before the <LOC> court.
**Significance of the outcome**: Prior to the effect, victims of sexual violence in <LOC> were reluctant to report assaults because of the social stigma associated with these crimes. This development is important for the objectives of the MDR programme, as it demonstrates significant progress towards holding perpetrators of sexual violence accountable and protecting the rights of victims in the <LOC> region.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: By encouraging the reporting of acts of violence, the programme helps to break the silence surrounding these crimes, thereby promoting access to justice and the emergence of a more informed and supportive community against gender-based violence.
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Individual
**Contribution of the programme**: The MDR project monitor made a direct contribution to change by helping the victim's family to draw up a complaint following the documentation of a case of rape in the <LOC> cercle in 2023. In collaboration with TRIJEUD's legal clinic in <LOC>, this intervention enabled the case to be brought before the courts, which is currently underway at the <LOC> court. This direct contribution from the MDR programme has fostered positive change by offering concrete support to the victim's family and facilitating the legal process to combat sexual violence in the region.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: In addition to the MDR project monitor, TRIJEUD's legal clinic played a key role in providing legal and judicial assistance to the rape victim. This contribution strengthened the process of bringing the case to court, providing specialist support that made a significant contribution to combating sexual violence in the region.
