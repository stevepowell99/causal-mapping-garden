**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, <person>, the head of the National Security Agency of Iraq, announced that the process for obtaining security clearance, essential for victims and survivors to receive compensation, will be simplified and expedited.
**Significance of the outcome**: Eligible families seeking compensation faced significant delays and obstacles in obtaining the necessary security clearance, a crucial part of their compensation application process, with approvals taking <redacted>. A shorter delay for receiving compensation is one of the intermediary goals of the advocacy strategy.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Mr. <person> response indicated a move towards streamlining the process. He stated that security permits for those affected by HR abuses—including martyrs, the missing, and the injured—should be processed within the same governorate. This means that the subcommittees responsible for compensation should coordinate with the National Security Directorates to obtain the necessary security clearances locally, without the need to forward these applications to the Martyrs Foundation - Terrorism Victims Department in <LOC>. This decentralized adjustment will facilitate a more efficient and responsive compensation process for the affected families.
