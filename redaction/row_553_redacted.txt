**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, the Department of Media and Communication at the Matyor Directorate in <person> revised their communication and social media protocols to better protect victims’ data and information during announcements and information dissemination. 
**Significance of the outcome**: Protecting victim data is vital to safeguard their privacy, dignity, and safety. Previously, the directorate’s announcements publicly shared victims’ names and information, risking further harm. After advocacy and capacity building for 12 communication and management staff, the new protocol ensures sensitive information is handled with care, preventing misuse or exposure. Strengthening data protection reinforces victims’ trust in institutions and supports ethical, responsible justice processes.
