**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, <person> experienced a qualitative shift in its legal support mechanisms and capabilities.
**Significance of the outcome**: Specifically, the team’s capabilities improved in the following areas: 1. Enhanced Legal Follow-Up: The team now applies a more systematic legal approach, including consistent, field-based follow-up with victims in coordination with field staff. 2. Deeper Legal Understanding of Violations: The training enhanced the team’s legal knowledge of various types of violations, which has improved the quality and accuracy of legal memos. 3. Diversification of Legal Tools: Instead of relying solely on individual advocacy, the team now uses a range of tools such as comprehensive legal memos submitted to relevant actors across different parties to the conflict. 4. Updated Monitoring and Documentation Forms: The legal documentation forms were revised to ensure more comprehensive and accurate information collection, which strengthened the quality of the data used to inform legal interventions. 5. Human-Centered Storytelling: Legal reports now incorporate the human stories of victims, highlighting the emotional and social dimensions of violations and enhancing the legal impact of the memos. 6. Tailored Legal Strategies: The team now develops individualized legal action plans for each case, tailored to the status of the victim—whether detained or forcibly disappeared—and identifying the best pathways to reach and assist them.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The result was an increase in legal documentation performance, as captured by Mwatana's internal documentation index: 1. A higher number of legal memos and human-interest stories produced. 2. Revised legal files and documentation templates.  In addition, key improvements since the implementation of the project, include:  Broader legal analysis of violations, with alignment to international legal frameworks. Higher-quality legal documents presented to relevant stakeholders. Enhanced capacity to prepare comprehensive legal files, including victim details, nature of violation, responsible party, and recommended legal actions. 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: Organisation
**Contribution of the programme**: The HURICAP program, funded by Amnesty International, enabled <person> to successfully enhance the capabilities of its team. The team's skills were improved through the development of manuals, training packages, ToT training workshops, and the establishment of a training unit. With these measures in place, <person> was able to effectively design capacity-building workshops for different target groups. These workshops were then implemented with funds from other donors.
