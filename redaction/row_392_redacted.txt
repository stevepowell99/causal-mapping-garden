**Outcome description**: In BF in 2023, the monitors were able to obtain on at least 2 occasions (26 <redacted>) the release of at least 25 people who had been kidnapped or arbitrarily arrested by Volunteers for the Defence of the Homeland in <LOC> in the <LOC> region 
**Significance of the outcome**: In the northern region, the Volunteers for the Defence of the Homeland are often responsible for the illegal arrest of people. Many of the people they have arrested have been victims of enforced disappearance. Others are later found dead.   By obtaining the release of these 25 people, the monitors have made it possible to protect their right to life.
