**Outcome description**: By <redacted>, relatives of people who were killed or missing during conflict in South Sudan were able to upload names of the loved ones directly to the website
**Significance of the outcome**: In the past names were only collected and documented by volunteers, not everyone was having access to contribute to the documentation. Now all people in South Sudan are having access to upload names to be documented. People of South Sudan acknowledge importance of memorialization and honoring the dead
**Additional information (use if needed) **: More people have access to internet, more companies are coming in, more people are having smart phones.
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: National
**Contribution of the programme**: One on one interaction of ROWL volunteers with relatives of the deceased. 5 radio programs on importance of memorialization. Regular press releases. Continuous update of names on the website. A user friendly website; A link was created on the website making it easier for people from the public to upload names on their own initiative.
