**Outcome description**: On <redacted>, a group of 23 youth boys from Sarafand Public School in the South of Lebanon, who participated in life skills sessions with ABAAD, organized a recreational day for displaced children and facilitated an awareness session for parents on sexual harassment in public spaces.
**Significance of the outcome**: The youth come from a conservative area in <LOC>, where young people are often discouraged from engaging in topics like protection, gender-based violence, or community support. Youth are usually not seen or encouraged as active contributors to social cohesion or public awareness efforts. Boys are often limited to traditional masculine roles.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: These students come from a conservative community where young men are often discouraged from engaging in social or protection-related roles, especially those involving women and children. Before the intervention, their involvement in such issues was minimal or non-existent.

**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
