**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, members of the Human Rights Defenders Network (RPDDH) and members of the Coalition of Women Human Rights Defenders (CFDDH) began using the kobotool box to collect data on human rights violations.
**Significance of the outcome**: Prior to this digitization session, human rights defenders from the RPDDH and CFDDH used loose sheets of paper, notebooks, and social media, which exposed them, as well as victims and their monitors, to security risks and also potentially exposed the collected data to state or military actors. The introduction to digitizing data tools in <LOC> and kobocollect enabled each member organization to generate a database, which it uses to collect data securely, generate reports safely and quickly, while preserving data confidentiality.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Following the training provided by DefendDefenders, SECHA provided human, material, and financial resources to train the human rights defenders of the RPDDH and CFDDH in the digitization of data collection tools, so that each participant could digitize these tools and generate their own database.
