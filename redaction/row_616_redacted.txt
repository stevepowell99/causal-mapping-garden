**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, following the presentation of a study report on the presence of children in mining quarries in the Bapere sector of <LOC> territory (DRC), the territory administrator of <LOC> committed to instructing the Mine Police to act to reduce the presence of children in mines.
**Significance of the outcome**: Before the study, the mine police did not take into account the presence of children in the mines. However, during our visit to the area for supervision, we observed a decrease, which was also confirmed by witness statements. This marks a shift from previous inaction to a formal promise of enforcement, indicating increased recognition of child protection concerns in mining zones.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: According to witness statements and colleagues in the field, the mine police have imposed a curfew in the Mangurejipa mines to control the presence of children. This has led to a decrease in the number of children in the mines.
