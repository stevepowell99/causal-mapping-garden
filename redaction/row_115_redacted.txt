**Outcome description**:  In <redacted> of 2022 and as a result of <redacted> of activism ABAAD was trending over the digital arena and generated a high level of engagement on social media. The campaign "NO SHAME NO BLAME" hashtag trended #1 on Twitter, received extensive media coverage, including prime-time news features on several TV stations and coverage in newspapers and online news outlets. The campaign gained support from influencers, KPOs, celebrities, journalists, and activists, enabling us to mobilize a wide audience and spread our message. 
**Significance of the outcome**: The social media campaign was able to reach a large audience and generate significant engagement, including support from influencers, celebrities, and activists. The fact that the campaign dominated the digital arena and was trending #1 on Twitter, as well as being featured in various media outlets, suggests that it had a significant impact in raising awareness about sexual assault and harassment that the community members especially women and girls were struggling in <redacted>. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The campaign's success is reflected in the fact that people 11,794,591 were reached online, received 6,042,112 views, and generated 339,611 engagements, including 94,486 likes and 12,037 shares on social media platforms.
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: National
**Contribution of the programme**: The social media campaign effectively engaged a wide audience in discussions about sexual assault and harassment. ABAAD's "No Shame No Blame" campaign provided a platform for social media users to speak out about their experiences, share information, and challenge harmful attitudes and behaviors related to gender-based violence. The program provided the necessary resources, including messaging, visuals, and social media strategies, to ensure the success of the campaign. Additionally, ABAAD collaborated with public figures and influencers to amplify the campaign's reach and impact. The program's contribution was critical in ensuring that the campaign achieved its objectives in sheding light, mobilizing and demanding amendments of the law.
