**Outcome description**: in 2023 the Women's Affairs Department (WAD) of the Bethlehem Government included the priorities of the Al-Manshiya sclr protection group in their own governorates priorities
**Significance of the outcome**: 8 members of the protection group reached out to the WAD and invited them for a visit. This was the first time the WAD visited the villages. 4 meetings took place resulting in the WAD taking over the priorities. Furthermore, later two of the protection group members were appointed as members in the Women's committee of the governorate recognising their own power as rightsholders. Also showing a shift in power relations, women taking an active role in local decision making bodies. This is an important point for the protection group, showing that objectives can be achieved. Furthermore, it also serves as inspiration for other protection groups. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Conservative and marginalized area, really a breakthrough in light of gender equality for the entire community
