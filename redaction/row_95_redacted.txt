**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, the Governor of <LOC> officially instructed the administrator of the territory of <person> to remove all road barriers from the Chiefdom of the Bashali.
**Significance of the outcome**: Free movement and a decrease in impoverishment in the community. Decrease in cases of arrests and the stresses that result from these roadblocks. The Provincial Governor reacted against these practices by giving written instructions to the Territory Administrator of <person> to remove the roadblocks in the <redacted> chiefdom.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Roadblocks are numerous and mainly owned by soldiers and armed groups. They constitute places of human rights violation and abuses of users of the roads.
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: Assodip organized an advocacy meeting in <LOC> on <redacted>, 2022. ASSODIP communicated in <redacted> the resolutions of this meeting to the local authority and copied to the Governor of the Province the resolutions of this meeting and shared informally this relationship with the Governor's Human Rights Advisor.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: The local authority subsequently convened a community-wide safety meeting around the issue. A list of roadblocks was established in this meeting and sent to the Governor. The latter wrote to the administrator of the Territory of Masisi to remove the roadblocks that there were in the <redacted> chiefdom.
