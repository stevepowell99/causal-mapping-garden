**Outcome description**: On <redacted>, 83 states including the Netherlands endorsed the Political Declaration on strengthening the protection of civilians from the humanitarian consequences arising from the use of explosive weapons in populated areas (EWIPA) in <LOC>. 
**Significance of the outcome**: The political declaration is the first formal recognition of the direct and indirect civilian harm caused by explosive weapons in populated areas. It commits states a.o. to avoid civilian harm by restricting or refraining from the use of explosive weapons in populated areas when civilian harm can be foreseen. It also commits states to provide victim assistance and collection and sharing of data on the use of explosive weapons. De declaration pursues a gender sensitive approach to victim assistance. 83 states have joined the declaration upon the signing day, including the Netherlands
