Kenya has court user committees that addresses issues of administration of Justice including identification needs of various agencies that hinder administration of justice. After the training in strategic advocacy, DCK supported HRD (<person>) to join the Court User Committees (CUC) for Kilifi country. In the CUC, <person> provided evidence to the Judge on how  Joel was in custody over fabricated cases and how he had been severally arrested multiple times because of his work on defending land rights in a salt mining area. The judge later passed a judgement exonerating <person> and a exemption from further arrest for his land rights work unless sanctioned by the ODPP.
