**Outcome description**: In <redacted>, a member of the Inamahoro movement living in Mahama refugee resettlement supported a victim of GBV to file a complaint to the local court, in collaboration with " Femmes Volontaires", a local association that fights against  Gender Based Violences after attending a training on Advocacy.
**Significance of the outcome**: For such cases, there was lack of solidarity and appropriate support from the camp authorities, family and community members coupled with limited knowledge and skills on the legal processes. The training on advocacy is important because it equips HRDs with skills on how to support victims of violence and advocate for them. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The member mobilised the camp authorities who showed solidarity to the victim by pronouncing that such cases should be reported to their office. The case is still being processed.
