**Outcome status**: Outcome
**OH-harvesting period**: harvest 1
**coutnry**: South Sudan
**Year**: 2022
**Outcome description**: In August 2022, the chairperson of the budget committee on finance and economic planning in the parliament  shared the budget documents (financials bill 2023, Appropriation Bill, Summary of budget allocation) for financial year 2022/23 with SSuDEMOP to review the budget and provide comments to  the budget structure that was initially proposed.
**Significance of the outcome**: Previous budget processes did not explicitly request for civic input to the budget structure. The meeting with the chairperson of the budget committee of the parliament signifies a commitment to an inclusive budget review process.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: National
**Contribution of the programme**: Under the SCC programe, SSuDEMOP conducted (1) one strategic meeting with the chairperson of the budget committee of the parliament. In July 2022, SSuDEMOP held a meeting with the chairperson of the budget committee of the parliament during which they shared an advocacy paper with a proposed list of items for tax exemption. This meeting was the second of its kind, because SSuDEMOP had once pushed for exemption of sanitary material. This led to the strengthening of a relationship with this critical stakeholder. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: 7 Civil Society Organisations representatives. The Civil Society Organizations representatives includes Women Advocate Association of South Sudan, Women for Justices and Equity South Sudan, South Sudan Women Empowerment Network and SSUDEMOP. According to the Budget and Economic Commission the chairperson does not have great powers to makes changes, he is only an entry point for the young women in accordance with the agreement in the R.ARCSS, the chair only waits for affirmation from the commission members. 
**Rating contribution**: great
**The change was unintended**: NO
**Evidence supporting the outcome statement**: Report of the strategic meeting with chairperson of the budget committee and advocacy paper containing a list of items proposed for tax drop, exemption and waiver for 22/23
