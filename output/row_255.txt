**Outcome description**: In November-December 2023, regional civil society forums held in the preparations to UNEA-6 pushed for the inclusion of conflicts' contribution to the triple planetary crisis in the UNEA's agenda.
**Significance of the outcome**: Conflicts have been featured both in the decisions of the UN Environmental Assembly and in the civil society input to UNEA agenda on multiple occasions before; however, having the recognition of conflicts' interplay with the triple planetary crisis at UNEA-6 was crucial as its theme was centered aroud effective and inclusive multilateral actions to tackle environmental challenges, thus, having potential to advance EPS programme objectives. In this regards, building coalitions with other civil society stakeholders during the regional consultations, and influencing the language of joint outcome documents, was a foundation for successful advocacy at UNEA-6 in 2024.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: early/small
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: International
**Contribution of the programme**: PAX played a leadership role in getting the conflict language reflected in the joint regional statements for UNEA-6 from the civil society, coordinated several advocacy initiatives, and conducted a session on conflicts in the international civil society discussions in preparations to UNEA-6.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Yes, a couple of other NGOs were pushing for the same objectives as PAX, but PAX played a coordination role. 
**Rating contribution**: great
