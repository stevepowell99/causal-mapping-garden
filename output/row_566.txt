**Outcome description**: On 5th July 2025, Governor of Central Equatoria State Gen. Rabi Mujung Emmanuel gave a support of  10,000,000  SSP and equivalent of 1700 USD financially to JMWOA during the commemoration of  the 33th anniversary of the Juba Massacre of 1992 at Nyakuron Culture Centre in Juba. This was the first time ever and a sign of support of and solidarity with the widows and  the families who lost their loved ones since 1992. The former governor of CES General Clement Wani alloted a plot size of 1600 square meter to JMWOA to built a memorial side (a room and signpost) for JMWOA. This can contribute to sustainability of JMWOA's presence and continued lobby for truthfinding and justice while commemorating the 1992 massacre also for the next generation.   
**Significance of the outcome**: Financial contribution of Central State Government to JMWOA during an annual commemoration event,  was a positive contribution and an indication of government opening up to see the past and work together with the vicitims to access justice for the victims.
