**coutnry**: Burundi

**Outcome description**: The Government of Burundi acknowledged receipt of ACHPR recommendations and promised to take corrective action during the review of Burundi by the Committee against Torture on November 2023.
**Significance of the outcome**: For a long time, the government of Burundi kept denying torture of victims in prisons. The conditions of detention in Burundi's prisons are bad and access to justice is limited. To fight against impunity, ACAT decided to do referral to international mechanisms for the protection of human rights of prisoners experiencing torture. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: There is impunity for the perpetrators of human rights violations, namely cases of torture and arbitrary detention.
**Rating significance**: early/small
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: National
**Contribution of the programme**: The information was collected in the framework of support of the project. Also the project supported advocacy, especially before the mechanisms of treaty bodies such as the UN Committee Against Torture, the UPR and the UN Human Rights Council, and in collaboration with the Special Rapporteur on the situation of human rights in Burundi.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Burundi's bar associations and a few NGOs such as AFJB (Association des Femmes Juristes du Burundi) on the ground have contributed to the judicial progress of irregular cases and judicial assistance. 
**Rating contribution**: moderate
**The change was unintended**: NO
