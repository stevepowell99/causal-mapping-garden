**Outcome description**: In October 2024 in Banjul, the Chairperson of the African Commission for Human and Peoples Rights was able to speak about the discrimination against LGBTIQ people in Kenya. 
**Significance of the outcome**: There has been a reduction in civic space and freedoms of expression in Kenya. In 2023, the Kenyan Parliament tabled the "Family Protection Bill" aimed at protecting the family against homosexuality and also a lot of attacks on the LGBTIQ community. So the ability of the ACHPR chairperson to speak about the discrimination was a step in calling on the Kenyan Government to respect and uphold rights of all persons.
