**Outcome description**: As a result of a training on Gender Integration with ABAAD staff (e.g. program managers, project coordinators, technical team members), staff were able to develop and submit subsequent project proposals that more effectively integrated a gender-sensitive and transformative lens by being more repsonsive, inclusive and responsive to the gender needs and gaps in the community. In addition, they were also able to share their knowledge and expertise from the training with other organizations and programs they manage, influencing them to adopt similar approaches.
**Significance of the outcome**: The gender integration training provided to ABAAD staff had a significant impact on their ability to use the toolkit for proposal development and influence other projects, programs, and organizations. The training equipped the staff with the tools, knowledge and skills necessary to integrate a gender lens  in their work, and to ensure that all ABAAD's programs and activities were gender-sensitive.
