**Outcome description**: In August 2023, 7 illegal military roadblocks out of 16  (subjecting civilians to illegal taxes and extortion) were closed by Colonel Bakole. 
**Significance of the outcome**: The effect is that military authorities acknowledge the HR violations and acted accordingly, through ordering the closure of the illegal roadblocks so that civilians can travel freely which is their human right. If one colonel does this, it might influence others as well, hence influencing the peace process.
