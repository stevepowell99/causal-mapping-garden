**Outcome description**: In January 2025, AMEL Association has enhanced its engagement of men in sexual and reproductive health (SRH) and gender-based violence (GBV) prevention by adopting innovative, participatory methods such as game-based awareness activities (“Play for Change”).
**Significance of the outcome**: This outcome represents an important shift in how AMEL Association designs and implements its programs by integrating innovative, participatory approaches to male engagement in SRH and GBV prevention. The change reflects a strengthening of the CSO’s capacity to deliver more inclusive and effective interventions, improving the quality and relevance of their programming.
