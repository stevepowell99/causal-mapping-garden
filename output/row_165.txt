**Outcome description**: In 2022, activists who participated in District Activist Academy implemented their own initiatives and follow-up actions. The initiatives included an activist creating a new podcast on civic rights in Jbeil; a filmmaker and activist from a conservative background in Tripoli creating new movies tackling women’s rights and politics; an activist reactivating the ‘Shift to Move’ initiative in Britel; and an activist starting an initiative to connect imprisoned youth with youth outside the prison.
**Significance of the outcome**: Before joining the District Activist Academy implemented by MAAN, activists did not know how to start advocacy initiatives. By strengthening their activism skill set and knowledge, they were able to identify what they can do within their scope of influence to further accountability in Lebanon with no resources.
