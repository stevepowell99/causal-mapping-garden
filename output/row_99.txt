**Outcome description**: In April 2022, 6 activists in Tripoli,  succeeded for the first time to meet with the mayor and the municipality council with other attendees from the public to present the needs of youth in the area and to advocate for budget transparency by the municipality.
**Significance of the outcome**: This is the first time youth activists meet with the municipality in Tripoli. The meeting took place following great resistance from the mayor to publicly show the municipal budget although it is legal.
