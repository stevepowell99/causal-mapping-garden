**Outcome description**: In July 2023, the Provincial Governor of North Kivu instructed the Sokola II Operational Sector Commander and the administrators of the Masisi and Nyiragongo territories (In letter no. 01/1122/CAB/GP-NK/2023, July 24, 2023) to identify illegal roadblocks, remove them and impose sanctions on recalcitrant offenders.  
**Significance of the outcome**: This effect, like the previous one, is significant in that it is an indication of the impetus given to the protection of human rights by the provincial authority. This is an evolution given that, as mentioned above, it was rare for the authorities to give clear written instructions to subordinates in stead of verbal instructions regarding the protection and respect of human rights 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: early/small
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: The program supports MDR activities and the use of this information in lobby and advocacy activities. In this context, ASSODIP writes letters, in this case to the Provincial Governor (no. 005/ASSODIP/2023, dated 03 April 2023), in which it was recommended to remove the roadblocks it had identified that infringed freedom of movement.
