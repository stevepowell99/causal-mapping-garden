**Outcome description**: On August 31, 2023, the Governor of Nineveh, Najm al-Jubouri, announced the distribution of lands to female survivors to improve their quality of life, and to open a center in Sinjar to facilitate the process of reparations for female survivors and provide them with basic services.
**Significance of the outcome**: Sinjar district suffers from marginalization and lack of attention, and survivors also need financial support and housing to improve their economic situation. There is no center in Sinjar to facilitate transactions and provide basic services to survivors. Also, the infrastructure in Sinjar is destroyed due to the war, and some of these structures did not exist in the first place. This contributes to improving their lives and increasing access to necessary services. The governor also said that there is an engineering effort directed by the Prime Minister to go to each of the following Al-Adnaniyah Al-Qahtaniyah complexes and to the northern district of Sinjar District to develop the infrastructure and provide a better environment

**Contribution of the programme**: On August 31, 2023, in the Nineveh Governorate, Mosul District, in the Engineers Union Hall, the Al-Masala Organization, in cooperation with the Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Antiquities, organized a forum to celebrate the National Day of Women Survivors and commemorate the victims of violence based on religion or belief, and under the slogan “In honor of those who left a mark on our hearts.. “Your memory is everlasting,” in the presence of the Minister of Culture, the Governor of Nineveh, the Director of Survivors’ Affairs, representatives of civil society organizations, civil activists, media figures, representatives of government departments, families of victims, survivors, and specialists in issues of peacebuilding and transformative justice. During his speech delivered by the Governor of Nineveh, he stated the distribution of lands to survivors to support survivors of difficult situations and improve their quality of life, and to open a center in Sinjar to facilitate the process of conducting transactions for survivors and provide them with basic services, which contributes to improving their lives and increasing access to necessary services. The governor also said that there is an effort An engineer was instructed by the Prime Minister to go to each of the following Al-Adnaniyah Al-Qahtaniyah complexes and to the northern district of Sinjar district to develop the infrastructure and provide a better environment.
