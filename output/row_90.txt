**Outcome description**: In August 2022, in Kindu, the director of the Lomami National Park signed a memorandum of understanding with the CDJP-Kisangani, allowing the latter to oversee mediation activities with communities living near the park.
**Significance of the outcome**: The population has confidence in the CDJP-Kisangani, a faith-based grassroots actor that conducts regular activities in the territories of Opala and Ubundu. The signing of the agreement by the park authorities was necessary to move forward in the dialogue process, in a context where tensions persist between ICCN and the neighboring communities. Previously, the local communities surrounding the Lomami National Park, and the armed opposition among the population, also accepted (the terms of) a dialogue process.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Non-state authorities
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: The CDJP-Kisangani invited ICCN to a workshop in Kisangani in 2021, which helped ICCN understand the importance of  CDJP's work around the park. Other meetings were subsequently held in Kinshasa (with the ICCN general direction) and Kindu (park headquarters).
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: No.
