**Outcome description**: During the validation of the Who's Next Report in December 2023, a Directorate of Criminal Investigation officer- Wesley Nyagat in Kwale has committed to take up the investigation into the case of extortion.
**Significance of the outcome**: This outcome is significant as this illiustrates a duty bearer openly committing to investigate the extortion case. This directly contributes to the realization of the objective  to stimulate state actors to respond and address the impact of the violations to the family of the victims
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Mzee Mwatabu is the father to a dissapeared victim AST. The ED occurred in October 2019. Two years after the dissapearance, In 2021, Mzee Mwatabu started getting calls from unknown people who demanded for money, in exchange for his son's return. Thus far he has sent Ksh.200,000 (about 1200,00 USD) via digital transactions. He still continues to get calls from the unknown people, with threats of killing his son if he reported the matter to the police. He never reported this extortion incident to the police before.
