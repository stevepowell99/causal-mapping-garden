**Outcome description**: On 16 May 2023, during the SPLA Day Celebrations, the governor of Central Equatoria and other Goverment Officials  approached  members of the Juba Massacre Widows Association seeking to be lead to the site the 1992 massacre, the willingness of the officials to learn more about the incident, commemorate with the widows and pay respect to the victims of the 1992 Juba Massacre opened a common  ground on dealing with the past.
**Significance of the outcome**: This is the first time after 32 years, that the government of South Sudan acknowledged and recognized the sacrifices of the victims of the 1992 Juba Massacre.
