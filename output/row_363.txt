**Outcome description**: In October 2023, a moderate number of families agreed to send more girls to formal education and vocational training centre in Kuron.
**Significance of the outcome**: The patriachal cultural setting of Toposa people has denied girls to attend formal education and vocational training for they are seen as source of wealth through marriage. This will lead to more empowerment of these girls and will make them self relient. This will also act as an example for other families who have indicated that next year they will also send their daugthers to school.
