**Outcome description**: By June 2025, Zakrayat, a 21-year-old Palestinian woman from Al-Beddawi, Lebanon, became a leading voice on taboo topics such as sexual and reproductive health, fostering more equal household dynamics and inspiring other girls to join similar activities.
**Significance of the outcome**: Zakrayat’s transformation reflects a meaningful shift in a conservative community where young women are often expected to remain silent on sensitive issues. By speaking openly about taboo topics and promoting shared household roles, she challenged traditional and restrictive gender norms in her community. Her ability to influence both her family and peers demonstrates early signs of broader social impact, aligning directly with the program’s goals of empowering women and fostering community-level change.
**Additional information (use if needed) **:  
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Individual
**Contribution of the programme**: Zakrayat joined sessions proivided by ABAAD after being invited by a friend who had experienced their value. The safe space provided by the program allowed her to express herself, reflect on her life, and access information often withheld from women in her context. The sessions helped her build confidence, assertiveness, and a sense of solidarity that enabled her to shift her behavior and inspire others.
