**coutnry**: Iraq
**Year**: 2022
**Outcome description**: In July 2022, the community police department of Bashiqa reported an unprecedented increase in the number of calls from women who were subjected to electronic harassment and extortion
**Significance of the outcome**: Women in Ninewa are particularly at risk of online harassment and extortion due to existing patriarchal norms, and this has been reportedly increasing over the last months. However women who were threatened online rarely ever reported their case for fear of repraisal. This further contributed to their marginalization.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: In July 2022, a group of volunteers from the city of Zeitoun, with the support of the Peace and Freedom Organization, produced a video to raise awarness about electronic blackmail and how to act in the event of exposure to it, highlighting the rights of women to come forward without consequences. It was done with the participation of religious leaders and trusted community figures in all languages, and it was highly promoted on social media and became viral online in the region.
