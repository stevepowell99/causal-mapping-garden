**Outcome description**: Dutch parliament engaged on compensation for Hawija in September 2021 through written questions by D66. 
**Significance of the outcome**: In December 2020, after a motion from the members of parliament Belhaj and Voordewind and with a reference to a PAX report, then Minister Bijleveld announced that the Netherlands would make 4 million dollars available “to the benefit of the community in Hawija, Iraq, who on the night of 2 on 3 June 2015 was hit by a Dutch weapon deployment.” After the publication of our September 2021 publication on Hawija, an update on the assistance to the community in Hawija was asked by D66 to the Minister.
