**Outcome description**: In December 2023, the National Transitional Legislative Assembly adopted the scenario report to be part of their policy in Juba. 
**Significance of the outcome**: This is the first time the National Transitional Legislative Assembly directly adopted a report from civil society into their policy to influence the debates and agendas within parliamant. Before the government received the scenario report, Concerned Citizens Network for Peace was seen as a national scurity threat. After receiving the document they had a change of perception about Concerned Citizens Network for Peace. From a threat to a group that can help shape the national policy of the government. There was a big ask for receiving more copies of the report.
