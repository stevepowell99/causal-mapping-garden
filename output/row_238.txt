**Outcome description**: In april 2023, 10 new (academic) volunteers (2 males and 8 females) joined Khazaeen increasing its capacity to organise events
**Significance of the outcome**: Khazaeen largely runs on volunteers. Having more volunteers therefore increases to possibilities to organise events and reach more people
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: early/small
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Individual
**Contribution of the programme**: PAX supported Khazaeen in organising an interactive workshop for 10 academics in its office about the importance of digitizing materials and the importance of digitizing their researches, articles, etc. they have worked in groups on how to digitize, how to write a content related to digitized materials and to briefly explain what the material is about. After the workshop all 10 asked to be part of Khazaaen Voluntary team and as a first activity organised a seminar in August.
