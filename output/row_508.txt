**Outcome description**: in September 2024, Dr. Aziz Alghashian was appointed as Mitvim’s first Saudi Policy Fellow, enhancing access to authentic Saudi perspectives.
**Significance of the outcome**: Mitvim’s appointment of Dr. Aziz Alghashian as its first-ever Saudi Policy Fellow marks a historic milestone for Israeli think tanks. Made possible through the groundwork laid by the PAX project, his addition revolutionized Mitvim’s engagement with the Gulf. He authored key policy papers such as 'Saudi Perspective: The Palestinian Issue Cannot Be Pushed Aside' and 'Exaggerations, Obstacles and Opportunities: The Saudi Arabian Position in the Gaza War.' Dr. Alghashian also represented Mitvim at its annual conference and other public initiatives, sharing direct Saudi insights with Israeli audiences.

This decision enabled Mitvim to directly introduce authentic Saudi voices into Israeli policy discourse countering efforts to reduce civic space
