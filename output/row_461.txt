**Outcome description**: On October 10, 2024, President of the Republic Evariste Ndayishimiye declared a presidential pardon for the release of prisoners in Burundi
**Significance of the outcome**: Several Burundian citizens, HRDs and Journalists have been arbitrarily arrested and detained without trial. This act contributed to a high rate of prison overcrowding. With the October 10, 2025, presidental pronouncement, there has been a release of many arbitrarily imprisoned prisoners and a reduction in prison overcrowding, which has led to an improvement in the conditions of detention of prisoners.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: It should be noted that prison overcrowding is high, at more than 13,000 inmates for a capacity not exceeding 4,500 places, which deteriorates the conditions of detention of prisoners: https://www.acatburundi.org/rapport-sur-le-monitoring-des-violations-des-droits-des-prisonniers-4/
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: National 
**Contribution of the programme**: The SCC program contributed through Human Rights Violations documentation/monitoring/alerts and reporting. The program partners have severally asked the president to release all political prisoners unconditionally. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Media and other Civil Society partners /Ministry of Justice.
