**Outcome description**: In October 2022, MINUSMA expressed its interest in better documenting cases of human rights violations outside SCC intervention zones by applying the Monitoring, Documentation and Reporting method, inviting ADD to submit a proposal to support MINSUMA in the application of Monitoring, Documentation and Reporting.
**Significance of the outcome**: MINUSMA is a large organisation with considerable resources. Its application of the SDR method could result in high-quality monitoring of the human rights situation and strong advocacy based on proven facts.  NB: In June the decision was taken to withdraw the MINUSMA mission. That information was not yet known when harvesting the outcomes.
