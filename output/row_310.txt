**Contribution of the programme**: On August 31, 2023, in the Nineveh Governorate, Mosul District, in the Engineers Union Hall, the Al-Masala Organization, in cooperation with the Ministry of Culture, Tourism and Antiquities, organized a forum to celebrate the National Day of Women Survivors and commemorate the victims of violence based on religion or belief, and under the slogan “In honor of those who left a mark on our hearts.. “Your memory is everlasting,” in the presence of the Minister of Culture, the Governor of Nineveh, the Director of Survivors’ Affairs, representatives of civil society organizations, civil activists, media figures, representatives of government departments, families of victims, survivors, and specialists in issues of peacebuilding and transformative justice. During his speech delivered by the Minister of Culture, he stressed the need to bring justice to the victims of all Iraqi components who have suffered over the past decades until now.
