**Outcome description**: In October 2023, UN Special Rapporteur on Human Rights to Water and Sanitation presented his report to UNGA on the water a tool for peace, which included the contributions of PAX and our local partners from Syria, Iraq and South Sudan.
**Significance of the outcome**: A dedicated report from the Special Rapporteur contributed to building the knowledge-base on the interlinkages between the environment, peace and conflict, and was helpful in bringing these issues to the attention of Member States at UNHQ - in line with what PAX has been advocating. Moreover, having a Special Rapporteur as a partner for pushing for the EPS-related agenda also heps to get greater attention to this issue. Another significant aspect of this report was the process of collecting evidence and information, which enabled local partners in conflict-affected partner to channel their messages to the highest UN body, which is crucial for objectives of the PoV programme.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: International community
**Scale**: International
**Contribution of the programme**: Positive and intended: PAX coordinated a joint submission to the SR's report, involving four partner organisations from Syria, Iraq and South Sudan. In October 2023, PAX organised an UNGA side event in New York on the topic of water insecurity, providing an additional platform for the Special Rapporteur to present the findings of his report.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Yes, many other organisations provided their inputs to SR's report.
**Rating contribution**: little
