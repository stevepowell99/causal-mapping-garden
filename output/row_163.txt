**Outcome description**: In October 2022, female peace actors from Rabiaa sub-district started to take action and participate in designing and implementing monthly activities within the local peace committee in Rabiaa sub-district in Ninewa.
**Significance of the outcome**: Ninewa in general considered as conservative community and Rabiaa sub-district is purely tribal community where female have very limited role and deprive it from their basic rights including the education. However, and its important through long-term interventions of PAX and its partner Al-Mesalla organization within this project, and by creating safe space and addressing the norms and harmful practice affecting women through supporting initiatives of the local peace committees in Rabiaa, there is noticeable change in the role of female and they have started to take effective role in the planning and implementing peace activities in their community.

**Contribution of the programme**: In October 2022, Female members of Local Peace Committee in Rabiaa have designed and implemented several sessions on peacebuilding and role of female, and hold various meetings with local authorities of Rabiaa to present and advocate on women issues in the district.
