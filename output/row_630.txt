**Outcome description**: In Burkina Faso, Mali, and Niger, since 2023, the CBDDH, COMADDH, and RNDDH use more secure communication tools.
**Significance of the outcome**: Before 2023, CBDDH, COMADDH and RNDDH used less secure communication tools in their daily activities. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Following the physical and digital security training held in November 2022 in Lomé, Togo, human rights defenders from Burkina Faso, Mali, and Niger and the digital security audits conducted in the offices of national HRDs Coalitions in Burkina Faso, Mali and Niger, CBDDH, COMADDH and RNDDH changed practices to use more secure communication tools such Secure Private Messenger and ProtonMail.
