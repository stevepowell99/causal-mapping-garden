**Outcome description**: In September 2024, States and other international actors expressed support for the EPS agenda at the side event to the Summit of the Future in New York, organized by PAX.
**Significance of the outcome**: PAX has been pushing for the recognition of the EPS (Environment, Peace and Security) issues throughout the negotiation process around the Pact for the Future -  a new global agreement for improving multilateral governance that was to be adopted at the Summit for the Future. Yet this did not materialise due to the opposition of some Member States. In response, PAX organized a high-level side event on the topic to draw international attention to the issue and to explore ways to establish the EPS agenda beyond the Pact process. Multiple States and IOs co-sposored the event and/or participated at it, delivering statements in support of greater attention to th EPS issues as part of the international governance improvements.
