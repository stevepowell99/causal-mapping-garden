**Outcome description**: In December 2024, In Arusha-Tanzania, Mr. Alaak Andrew of the South Sudan constitution working group was elected speaker of East African Community youth parliament where the youth of EAC shape decisions on matters affecting EAC members such as illegal visa fees at borders and capacity building for youth in enterprise development
**Significance of the outcome**: This is significant because the youth are in regional positions where they are able to influence the other regional leaders to lobby for changes in South Sudan especially relating to the implementation of the constitution making process and peace agreement
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Members of the South Sudan Youth Constitution working group have increased capacity to advocate for changes in South Sudan at regional level.
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: International 
**Contribution of the programme**: The program provided the youth with mentorship and coaching by training them in leadership, advocacy and public speaking. The program also provided the members with seed grants to finance theirr awareness raising initiatives on the constitution making process
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: UNMISS and UNDP also lobby for the enactment of the R-ARCSS and constitution making process
