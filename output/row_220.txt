**OH-harvesting period**: harvest 2
**coutnry**: DR Congo
**Year**: 2023
**Outcome description**: Between June and December 2023, three perpetrators in Rutshuru, Nyiragongo, and Beni were imprisoned (one soldier, a non identified person and M23 elements) who were accused of sexual violence  
**Significance of the outcome**: Before the intervention, victims of GBV were not aware of their right to health and local authorities did not necessarily take corrective mesures imprisoning perpetrators. Awareness raising campaigns in the Rutshuru territory (currently surrounded by several armed groups) on the risks of disease after sexual violence lead to women taking action leading to the imprisonment of the perpetrators and the womens treatment in hospital. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Individual
**Contribution of the programme**: Monitors are trained and supported in the programme on MDR and they used the information to advocate for the imprisonment of the perpetrators and to refer the victims. Also adocacy campaigns of UFAREP lead to change of victims' attitudes. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: In some cases there has been collaboration with DFJ (Dynamics of Women Jursists/Dynamique des Femmes Juristes). 
**Rating contribution**: great
**The change was unintended**: NO
**Evidence supporting the outcome statement**: Monitoring reports of UFAREP
