**Outcome description**: During a briefing by the Refugee Protection Watch coalition (RPW) for the Dutch Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) in March 2024, the MFA stated they would publish a new grant directly for Refugee Led Organisations in 2023. The Dutch MFA also highlighted that this new grant is a result of the advocacy efforts of RPW.
**Significance of the outcome**: One of RPW's strategic objectives is promoting the role & work of Refugee-Led Organizations (RLOs) among international donors. This outcome demonstrates that the Dutch government remains a key ally and donor for Syrian refugee protection efforts. Also, it shows that there is increased awareness amongst the donor community on the importance of resourcing Refugee Led Organisations (RLOs).
