**Outcome description**: Two participants in Hadramout got grants and support from their local councils to expand their peacebuilding initiatives such as fixing lights in the streets, fixing some roads and enrolling disabled people in Hadramout University. 
**Significance of the outcome**: There was a lack of the space and plaform for youth and women to present their local peacebuilding efforts. R Talks was a platform for them to present their peacebuilding efforts and gain more exposure, therefore better connections and more support from local councils. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: 200 people attended the Rtalks event in Hadramout, which was organised by Resonate Yemen. More than 1 million people interacted in the R Talks social media campaigns, advocating for and discussing local peacebuilding, conflict resolution and social cohesion. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: No other actors contributed to this outcome.
