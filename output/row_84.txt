**Outcome description**: In 2022, many displaced women, after sharing among themselves their memories, experiences and traumas, showed interest to write their own stories of forced displacement
**Significance of the outcome**: During the project led by Women Now, women having similar experiences came together in a safe space and with the support of specialists, they were encouraged to speak up and share traumatic experiences, emotions and memories. While women felt beforehand isolated and alone, they then felt a sense of belonging to a group and felt acceptance., filling a void left by the loss of their social circles due to war and displacement.  Many women were further encouraged to write their own stories in order to share them further. Women were empowered to start their writing journey. Women found a platform where their voices could be heard. Women expressed a desire to provide an alternative story, not dominated by mainstream narratives, but rather one reflecting their own unique perspective and experience.  Through writing they were able to release grief, fears, memories, thoughts . freeing themselves from the burden of silence and isolation.
