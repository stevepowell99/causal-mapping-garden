**Outcome description**: In 2022, following the training in gender integration and Prevention of Sexual Exploitation and Abuse, Accountability towards the affected population and prevention of corruption case for 15 Civil Society Organizations, they were able to incorporate issues of gender and accountability for gender based violence in program design, application of gender balance in overall activities and registered aphone number for safeguarding and assigned a staff to respond to complaints.  
**Significance of the outcome**: Before the training, Civil society Organizations lacked tools for accountability, gender integration, and prevention of sexual exploitation and abuse mechanisms. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The trainings also allowed Civil Society Organisations to create space to analyze how accountability mechanism, sexual exploitation and accountability impact their own programs and the contexts where they work. In addition, the training provided participants with tools to integrate accountability in their project cycle management. Sarah Justice from All developed a gender policy for the institution.
