**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: International community

**Contribution of the programme**: After the launch of the One Youth One Voice campaign on Facebook and WhatsApp in November 22, 2023, followers of South Sudan Youth Constitutional Working Group significantly increased on Facebook. The change is also a result of our other activities such as the our one-day training for youth peace ambassadors on civic engagement and public consultation on the constitution making process done in October 2023 at Juba Regency Hotel and the 2-day Youth Forum on developing a federal constitution held on 6th and 7th December 2023 at Crown Hotel, Juba. The project contributed greatly to this increase in youth membership in South Sudan Youth Constitutional Working Group.
