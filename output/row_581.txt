**Outcome description**: In 2024 and 2025, on several occasions, the UN OHCHR published positions that outlined specifically and clearly that companies as well as financial institions financing these companies are under obligation by international standards to act to prevent, mitigate and remediate violations of human rights and IHL in Gaza, and at risk of becoming legally liable if they failed to do so.
**Significance of the outcome**: In 2022, the UN Working Group on Business and Human Rights for the first time explicitly stated that the arms industry should conduct human rights due diligence. During 2024 and 2025, triggered by events in Gaza, the OHCHR and the Working Group published statements that emphasized this much more and urgently.
