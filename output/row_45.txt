**Outcome description**: 16 human rights defenders from the areas in North Kivu that were attacked by armed group M23 were able to protect themselves against the violence that specifically targeted these monitors during June/July 2022, September 2022 and January 2023. These HRD manage to organise a safe escape and temporarily moved out of their communities. 
**Significance of the outcome**: Before, monitors in the same situations could not protect themselves and were therefore subjected to violence from armed groups.
