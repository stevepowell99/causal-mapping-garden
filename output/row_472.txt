**Outcome description**: In November 2024,  two students committees were formed that focus on creating GBV awareness and they continue to meet on monthly basis to discuss and address issues of GBV.
**Significance of the outcome**: Bringing school children in presence of officials in education and law enforcement agencies to receive knowledge on pressing issue of early and enforced child marriage was very important for the school children to report on cases and to ensure towards collaborative efforts to address the vice. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: This is to enable the school children to becoem peer educators amongst themselves.
**Rating significance**: early/small
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Regional
**Contribution of the programme**: high, since for the first time WAAS with technical support of PAX is raising general awareness on a critical lass regarding important judicial matters.
