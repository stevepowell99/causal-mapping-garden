**Outcome description**: In June 2025, the municipal council in Brih-Chouf, Lebanon, adopted the environmentalist and youth-oriented agenda set by youth activists within the council.
**Significance of the outcome**: Supporting youth in being part of the decision-making process is the core goal of DAA and to open civic spaces in these particular regions that has been traditionally marginalized in politics
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: MAAN has created educational spaces for youth to discuss, understand, and develop needs assessments on multiple issues including social, economic, environmental and political spheres. MAAN has provided educational spaces and knowledge on civic and political rights, campaigning and digital campaigning and decentralization for activists to build campaigns and develop strategies for mobilization, communication, and agendas building for municipal councils.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Activists were also engaged in environmental campaigns in their regions before being elected. The campaigns were successful and have gained trust of the people on environmental issues.  Negatively: It was hard to mobilize activists because of the economic situation in the country, the disappointment of youth in politics and the absence of momentum to give them the security they needed to face traditional powers in conservative regions.
