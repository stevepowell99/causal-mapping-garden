**Outcome description**: After publishing our partner Al Mesalla's book on victims' rights and reconciliation in June 2025, the Peacebuilding Academic Center and Mosul University  initiated nine academic research projects on justice issues.  
**Significance of the outcome**: Developed from papers presented during the peacebuilding conferences organized by Al-Mesalla in 2024, this book is a collaborative product between Al-Mesalla and the Peacebuilding Center at the University of Mosul, following two years of collaboration. This book has become a credible reference for academic and policy discussions around justice and peace in Ninewa. It helped revive institutional interest in the themes of reconciliation and combating violent extremism, especially in post-conflict contexts. The nine academic research projects are the first of their kind.

**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Yes, the Peacebuilding Center at Mosul University played a significant role by co-developing the book’s content, facilitating academic collaboration, and mobilizing faculty and students to engage in research on justice-related themes.
