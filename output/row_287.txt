**Outcome description**: On 4th August 2023, community leaders were directed to report cases of sexual gender based violence to court of law. The declaration was done in the assembly meeting hall in Malakal town.
**Significance of the outcome**: During the last war in 2013 in Malakal town and around issues of sexual gender based violence were reported and perpurtrators are not held accountable but now some cases are being reported and the mobile court s looking into most cases. After women cacus in the Upper Nile state transitional legislative assembly received training on sexual gender based violence concepts conducted by Justice and Peace Commission- Catholic Diocese of Malakal, members of the assembly have advocated for awareness and women rights among women groups in Malakal and in the Protection site for civilians. Before the declaration there was little movement between towns and this declaration has given more trust between people and even women are moving on the streets more.
