**Outcome description**: On 19th July 2023, a youth union was formed by Malakal youth and youth of the protection of civilians site in Malakal.
**Significance of the outcome**: For the first time since 2016 incident in Protection of Civilians site the youth group from Dinka on one side and Shilluk-Nure on the other side gather together in Malakal town to discuss issues of peaceful co-existence. The formation of the union shows a lot of potential of cooperation between these groups in the future. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: Justice and Peace Commission of the Catholic Diocese of Malakal facilitated a youth roundtable meeting for both youth representatives of the Malakal and Protection of Civilian site. This meeting took place at the Ministry of Information and Telecommunication and services of the Upper Nile State Malakal. There were representatives of the Ministry present at the meeting which shows importance of the meeting. This meeting has caused for the creation of this union.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: The willingness of the representatives of the youth is an important contribution. The chair person of the different youth groups have played a big role in the creation of the union.
**Rating contribution**: moderate
