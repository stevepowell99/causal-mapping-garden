**Outcome description**: In October 2022, the tribe leaders in Maarib city gave the students of Saba University a temporary building as well as a land to build student housing for next year in Maarib city. 
**Significance of the outcome**: The road to Maarib is very dangerous because of ongoing conflicts and clashes between different armed groups. It is frequently blocked by Ansar Alla. Therfore, students have to skip classes or stop studying for security reasons. Having a student house close to the university will make it possible for students to have uninterupted study as well as to feel safe and stable. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The leaders of tribes collected the money and gave it as a gift to students to build the student housing. 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Non-state authorities
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: The project trained 12 women in advocacy, after that they could train 120 youth in advocacy. As a result of this training , a group of the trained youths in Maarib organized an advocacy campaign and had meetings with the tribe leaders to look into solutions and request support for studnet housing. The role of tribe leaders is essential in Maarib in supoorting their communites so they decided to allocate money to build the student hosuing.
