**Outcome description**: In December 2022, Azad Hassan became an official member of the PoV network of advocates in Ninewa to work towards advocating for the rights of marginalized community members in his community
**Significance of the outcome**: Azad Hassan, is a survivor of human rights violations at the hands of ISIS. He is of the Sunni Arab component and the common stereotype in Ninewa is that members of this community are supporters of the existence of ISIS. His inclusion in advocacy work on justice for victims in Ninewa is a significant step forward in achieving inclusive representation in the governorate.
**Additional information (use if needed) **:  Upon joining the network he told his story to a wide audience and shared with everyone how every component were affected by the war. Partners reported that audience members were shocked.  Azad had his hand cut off by ISIS militants and his family members were killed during the invasion.
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Individual
**Contribution of the programme**: Through the project, a safe space was provided for those affected by ISIS, in the form of a forum in the station hall of the city of Mosul. Approximately 150 people from different components and 6 television channels were invited by PFO and Azad's speech was broadcasted all over the governorate.
