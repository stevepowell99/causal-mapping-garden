**Outcome description**: In November 2024, Ten (10) women were added to the existing Peace 50 members Committee in Kuron that was mostly dominated by men (45 men 5 women) 
**Significance of the outcome**: The Peace Committee in Kuron was comprising of 45 men and 5 women before the addition of the 10 women. This was because women were mostly disregarded and not considered in the community forums. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The Patriachal nature of the Toposa people with male dominancy has always sidelined women in most community affairs. With the addition of the 10 women, it gave voice and representation of women in the community.
