**Outcome description**: In May 2022, the UNSC published their Protection of Civlians report that included a segment on  protection of the environment, which PAX was requested to provide input on environment, peace and security
**Significance of the outcome**: Growing recognition in the UNSC debates and among UN Member States that conflict-linked environmental damage is linked with peace and security through the Inclusion of relevant case-studies and EPS events in the UNSC
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: International community
**Scale**: International
**Contribution of the programme**: The inclusion of environmental issues by the UN(SC) in the PoC report in 2022, initiated by PAX, as well as getting support from States in setting up side-events around this theme and inclusion of EPS language in statements in UNSC  shows a recognition of of the EPS theme and links this with protection of civilians in the UN system and mainstreams the topic in the wider discussion around peace and security
