**Outcome description**: Throughout the Training of Teachers sessions in 2022 in North Beqaa, teachers verbally expressed that they will use the offline and online tools shared by Peace of Art to make their learning more impactful once schools reopen in North Beqaa. 
**Significance of the outcome**: In 2022, there were multiple teacher protests and school closures due to salaries not being sufficient to cover basic needs. Many teachers lost motivation to teach and these sessions brought back their motivation. Also, there is a lack of capacity building for teachers in Northern Beqaa and this activity allowed them to develop their skills and confidence which directly impacts how and what they teach young people.
