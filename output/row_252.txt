**Outcome description**: Between July and October 2023, 5 people in Beni, Miriki, Lubero and Rutshuru were released by the police and military who were arbitrary arrested and illegally detained 
**Significance of the outcome**: The effect shows  that due to lobby on HR, authorities do very slowly start respecting the law. These 5 persons did not violate of law but did not pay a small debt, had an argument with a husband, and didvnot participate in forced work which are no reasons to arrest and detain them. Their release is significant in the fact that they were released at the first place.
