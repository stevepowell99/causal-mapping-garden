**Outcome description**: In June 2025, during a mission to Ireland, the Community Action Centre (CAC) was, for the first time, invited to contribute to a parliamentary discussion on Ireland’s role in the authorization of Israeli bonds within the European Union system. Dr. Nuseibah, representing CAC, submitted a written and oral statement to the relevant parliamentary committee. The statement is expected to inform ongoing debates in Ireland and potentially influence broader EU policy on the authorization of Israeli war bonds.
**Significance of the outcome**: This reflects a new level of trust and recognition of CAC as a credible policy advisor to foreign duty bearers, including within the European Union.The invitation to participate in a national parliamentary process, particularly on an issue with EU-wide implications, signals CAC’s growing role in shaping international accountability mechanisms. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: This invitation marked a significant milestone in CAC’s international advocacy work. The intervention took place in the context of growing scrutiny over European financial instruments that may support Israel’s military actions. CAC’s contribution was made possible through its increasing visibility and credibility as a legal and policy actor. The statement prepared by Dr. Nuseibah addressed the legal and human rights implications of authorizing such bonds and was formally submitted to the committee for consideration in its deliberations.
**Rating significance**: early/small
**Social actor**: International community
**Scale**: International 
**Contribution of the programme**: The programme contributed to this outcome by supporting CAC’s advocacy missions and strategic engagement in Europe.
