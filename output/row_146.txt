**Outcome description**: During the year 2022, 8 youth volunteer teams in Bashiqa and Hamdaniya implemented 20 transformative justice initatives that included gender-sensitive and human rights approaches.  
**Significance of the outcome**: At the start of the year, the 8 volunteer teams had very little knowledge around gender inclusivity and human rights and focused mainly on the humanitarian needs of dominant groups in their respective communities. Through their increased capacities and sensitivity, their implemented activities allowed for an increased participation of marginalized community members (especially female survivors) and enabled their access to transformative justice processes.
