**Outcome description**:  In January 2023, the Independent Policing Oversight Authority (IPOA) pledged to work with HSI in addressing human rights violation cases and also join HSI during the upcoming human rights desks and make community awareness on the importance of reporting of cases.
**Significance of the outcome**: Given its mandate to investigate human rights violations allegedly perpetrated by state actors, IPOA as a key duty bearer has began initial conversations on collaboration with HSI, which may lead to investigations and ultimately redress for victims of HRVs.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: IPOA staff suggested to hold a meeting with both IPOA's and HSI' leadrship and further agree on ways to collaborate. It is the first time that IPOA reaches out to HSI in Garissa.
