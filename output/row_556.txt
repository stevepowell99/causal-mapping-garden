**Outcome description**: In April 2025, Iraqi partner Al-Mesalla and the Network of Advocates produced three policy papers with targeted recommendations based on 45 documented cases of human rights violations across Mosul, Sinjar, and Telafar. 
**Significance of the outcome**: This marks a shift from individual case documentation to policy-level advocacy, translating the MDR cases that was gathered by the Iraqi partners and network of advocate, to translate survivor voices and field data into actionable recommendations. The papers offer government actors and decision-makers concrete steps to address procedural and systemic barriers in the compensation process. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: Organisation
**Contribution of the programme**: AINL supported the capacity building and data collection for the MDR process carried out by Iraqi partners in 2024, resulting in two MDR reports. In 2025, PAX built on this foundation by training Al-Mesalla, PFO, and their network of advocates on advocacy-based evidence. This support enabled the translation of the MDR findings into three policy briefs aligned with victims’ demands, which are now being used to strengthen ongoing advocacy campaigns.
