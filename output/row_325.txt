**Outcome description**: The MDR training workshops, delivered by Mwatana's team to other CSOs in Q1-2023, enabled Mwatana to expand its network, get to know more CSOs at the national level, increased collaboration, something which was unlikely in previous years due to the war situation in the country.
**Significance of the outcome**: Organizations in various Yemeni governorates face restrictions and arbitrary practices by conflicting parties, such as control, movement restrictions, limited expression, access to information, and administrative constraints, so building relationships with local organizations is challenging but very significant in particular for advocacy and awareness raising efforts.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: After delivering MDR training workshops to other CSOs and starting the relationship with them, Mwatana conducted two community awareness raising meetings for the participating CSOs and a capacity buildig workshop in coordination with PASS organisation in Aden in September 2023..
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: Organisation
