**Outcome description**: Because of the exposure R Talks gave to the participants, in April 2022, media channels and TV radios for the first time invited 1 speaker of R Talks to talk and advocate about his work in peacebuilding
**Significance of the outcome**: Lack of public events where peacebuilders and activists in Yemen can share their local peacebuilding efforts and conflict resolution. Before the R Talks event, their work was limitedly known, and now they have more chances/opportunites like talking through media channels to tell about their work and advocate for peacebuilding.

**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Media
**Scale**: Local
