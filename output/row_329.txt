**Outcome description**: At the end of a conference in Kampala organized by PAX and APRu on June 14 and 15, 2023, with delegations from Uganda, CAR, DRC and South Sudan, the players involved signed a report presenting a political, logistical and financial framework for the joint DDRRR process.
**Significance of the outcome**: Following the commitment of the Ugandan and CAR governments, it was necessary to formalize this commitment and obtain a detailed plan for the DDRR process, with well-defined responsibilities. The report was also needed to secure MINUSCA's commitment to logistical support.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: It was the first time in more than 5 years that the governments of Uganda and CAR met in an official diplomatic meeting on a subject related to conflict and military issues. The participation of the three countries and their signing of the report was an important political issue.
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: Regional
**Contribution of the programme**: PAX and APRu organized a conference with the governments concerned (DRC, CAR and Uganda), chaired by the Ugandan Minister of Defense, which resulted in an agreement signed between these governments and PAX and APRu on the conditions and a roadmap for the DDRR process.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Yes, the Ugandan Minister of Defense chaired the conference. But the organization, preparation and financing were carried out by PAX and APRu.
**Rating contribution**: great
**The change was unintended**: NO
**Evidence supporting the outcome statement**: PAX and APRu reports, invitations, photos and signed agreement.
