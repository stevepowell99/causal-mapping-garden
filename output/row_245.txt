**Outcome description**: On 19th July 2023, 32 members of youth from Malakal and protection of civilians site, in presence of two ministers of Peacebuilding and Information, agreed publically that they will denounce violence and live peacefully in Malakal town.
**Significance of the outcome**: For the first time since 2016 incident in Protection of Civilians site the youth group from Dinka on one side and Shilluk-Nure on the other side gather together in Malakal town to discuss issues of peaceful co-existence.

**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Individual
**Contribution of the programme**: Justice and Peace Commission of the Catholic Diocese of Malakal facilitated a youth roundtable meeting for both youth representatives of the Malakal and Protection of Civilian site. This meeting took place at the Ministry of Information and Telecommunication and services of the Upper Nile State Malakal. There were representatives of the Ministry present at the meeting which shows importance of the meeting.
