**Outcome description**: In May 2023, in Kampala, the Ugandan Defense Minister signed a letter expressing the Ugandan government's commitment to the process of disarmament, demobilization, repatriation, reintegration and resettlement (DDRRR) of LRA elements in CAR and to a tripartite meeting in Kampala to organize this.
**Significance of the outcome**: After the LRA's commitment (see above), Uganda's clear commitment was a second key step towards the success of the DDR process.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: National
**Contribution of the programme**: The LRA groups' request had been discussed by PAX and APRu with the governments of Uganda, CAR and DRC, who indicated their initial support for such a demobilization process. In May 2023, a meeting was held in Kampala with the Ugandan Minister of Defense, during which the organization of a tripartite CAR-DRC-Uganda meeting was discussed in order to reach a common framework for such an operation. The Minister's letter followed this meeting in May 2023.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: No
**Rating contribution**: great
