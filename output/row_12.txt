**coutnry**: South Sudan
**Year**: 2021
**Outcome description**: In Sept. 2021 Toposa of Nabwelangakinei, Kapoeta East County returned eight recovered calves belonging to Murle.
**Significance of the outcome**: Prior to PoV intervention, raided cattle of the three communities were not recovered and returned. Return of raided cattle was one of the resolution of the June 2021 peace agreement.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The condition of the peace agreement where a penalty was levied on culprits to bring twice the raided livestock is a deterrent to raiding.
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Non-state authorities
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: HTPVK dessiminated information from victims of the raided cattle to the peace communities and community police. HTPVK relaid the information about the recovered animals back to the Murle to avoid revenge of raiding the Toposa animals.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: The 2021 peace agreement has played a vital part in this development.
**Rating contribution**: great
**The change was unintended**: NO
**Evidence supporting the outcome statement**: Yes, the partner reports and the 2021 peace agreement.
