**Outcome description**: Economic, social and cultural rights in Jerusalem included in submissions and presentations of Special Rapporteur Francesca Albanese. Including the inclusion of victim voices. (for example this document published September 21: https://www.un.org/unispal/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/A.77.356_210922.pdf)
**Significance of the outcome**: New practise, Special Rapporteur (SR) started in May 2022 so therefore no previous relation. CAC invested in the relation by arranging meetings and visits. CAC also arranged online sessions between the SR and Palestinians who fell victims to human rights violations in Jerusalem (5 victims in total, including the governor of the Palestinian Jerusalem governorate). Specific report will be published in 2023. CAC managed to establish a good working relation with the SR, relevant for future interventions
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: International community
**Scale**: International
**Contribution of the programme**: Part of the programme and discussed and agreed upon to use time and resources to invest in the relation. Financial support for CAC to cover the travel costs to the meeting as well as preparation. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Also other Palestinian organisations are in touch with the SR. 
**Rating contribution**: great
