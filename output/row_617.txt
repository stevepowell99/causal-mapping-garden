**Outcome description**: In January 2025, the local authorities in Bentiu of Unity state conducted transitional judicial services to both the communities in the protection of civilian sites and the town. 
**Significance of the outcome**: With the change of local leadersip in the state every six (6) months, this creates a gap for provision of judicial services as already capacitated old local authority in Unity state are removed from office.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Supporting local level awareness raising were conducted on transitional justice mechansm

**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: Regional
**Contribution of the programme**: The conducted training to the newly appointed local authorities on Transitional Justice Mechansm in Unity State.
