**Outcome description**: In June 2022, the Protestant Lay Ministry (MILAPRO), the Catholic Lay Apostolate Council of Congo (CALCC), and the Lay Coordinating Committee (CLC) signed a charter creating an early warning system for human rights defenders in Kinshasa.
**Significance of the outcome**: This was a new development that addressed a critical need for protection for human rights defenders linked to the networks of these three organizations in Kinshasa. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Their mobilization work-including popular marches-had drawn the attention of the authorities to these activists and increased the threats against them.
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Non-state authorities
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: In 2022, DD-with support from PAX-organized a training in Kinshasa on physical and digital security for CALCC, MILAPRO and CLC leaders. Several months later, DD returned to Kinshasa to follow up on this training.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: No, the creation of the early alert network is an effect directly related to the training with DD and follow-up activities undertaken by CALCC, MILAPRO and CLC; no other actors (outside of the project) contributed to this change.
**Rating contribution**: great
**The change was unintended**: NO
**Evidence supporting the outcome statement**: Signed charter; minutes of meetings following the signing of the charter.
