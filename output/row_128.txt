**Outcome description**: In November 2022, following RNDDH's plea to amend the decree on NGOs, the Nigerien government amended the decree on NGOs No. 2022-182/PRN/MAT/DC and to remove the clauses limiting the operations of NGOs. 
**Significance of the outcome**: In February 2022, the government promulgated a decree on NGOs with clauses restricting their operations. RNDDH initiated advocacy actions with the government of Niger to amend the unfavourable clauses.  Following RNDDH's continued advocacy, the government of Niger organised a national meeting to discuss the NGO decree with NGOs and amended the decree in accordance with the NGOs' suggestions. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: The government has set up a committee to review the decree. 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: State authorities
**Scale**: National
**Contribution of the programme**: The programme, through RNDDH, supported advocacy efforts on the NGO decree and also rallied NGOs to support the amendment of the law.  
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: The Government of Niger, ICNL, supports the decree review committee, USAID/SHIGA supports the draft legal arguments. 
**Rating contribution**: great
