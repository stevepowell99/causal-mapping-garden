**Outcome description**: In 2024, in Northern Bahr El Ghazal State women groups participated in Radio shows and at public gatherings to talk about Gender Equality, Law enforcement and protection of girls and women in a very sentisitve context. 
**Significance of the outcome**: Enhances women's roles in peace processes and increases representation in decision-making affecting their communities. Due to the Radio talk shows and public gatherings more people started to have conversation about the issues. The women receive more invitations from radio shows to talk about it, leading to more and more awareness about the issues. The fact that the women participated in radio shows in a delicate context is an outcome by itself
