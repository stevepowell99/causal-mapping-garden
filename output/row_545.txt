**Outcome description**: By July 18, 2025, a committee of 10 women in Al-Fakeha, Baalback, Lebanon elected in  leadership roles, developed a plan to present a social initiative to the municipality, and started organizing regular meetings to plan a community rehabilitation center offering multiple therapies through volunteer professionals.
**Significance of the outcome**: This outcome represents an important community-level shift from passive engagement to active leadership and service provision planning by women in a traditional area. It reflects enhanced local governance capacity and social cohesion, directly addressing community needs through self-organized solutions.
