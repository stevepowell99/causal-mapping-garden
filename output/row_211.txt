**Outcome description**: In February 2022, Dutch pension fund PFZW indicated by email that they had divested from Indian arms producer Bharat Electronics, over its arms supplies to Myanmar.
**Significance of the outcome**: While the pension fund hasn't yet improved its policy, this shows that the fund understand and accepts that the company in question is acting socially harmful.

**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Companies
**Scale**: Organisation

**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Another CSO (Justice for Myanmar) published the actual investments of the fund. 
**Rating contribution**: moderate
**The change was unintended**: NO
