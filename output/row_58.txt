**Outcome description**: Throughout 2022, there has been an increase in men from the community attending the SADA’s playback theater performances in Tripoli.
**Significance of the outcome**: The community in Tripoli which Bayt SADA targets through its activities is not exposed to many cultural and artistic activities. Men in these communities are quite traditional and do not usually share their experiences and personal stories in these artistic spaces. Therefore, the involvement of men in these performances is a new concept for the community.
