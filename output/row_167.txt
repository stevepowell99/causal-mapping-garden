**Outcome description**: Dr Sahabo Christophe was arrested on April 1st 2022 at Kira Hospital in Bujumbura. After more than 50 days in illegal detention at the Intelligence Services, he was granted access to a lawyer, visitation by familly and eventually transferred to prison following the open letter sent to the president.
**Significance of the outcome**: By law suspects are to be detained at police however in Burundi they are detained at the Intelligence services. Following the letter written by CAVIB , the President granted Dr. Christophe Sahabo public hearing and transfferred him to central prison. This shows progressive steps in adherence to international human rights standards on detainment.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Dr Sahabo specialised in opthomology in Switzerland. Following a call for investment in Burundi from President Nkurunziza, Dr Sahabo and his Swiss colleagues opened up a Hospital in 2014 with the majority stakeholders (47%) being the Swiss, private partners (21) and the government holding a small percentage (32%). With the success of the hospital, the governemnt wanted to take control of the hospital in 2022. They accused the doctor of embezzlement and falsifying agreements which led to his arrest.
