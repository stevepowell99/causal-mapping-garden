**Outcome description**: In December 2023, as experts of women particpation in peacebuilding, The CAP was asked by Sawt Al-Watan newspaper, a monthly newspaper specialized in Women's affairs, to take part in preparing the content and provide the necessary consultations for the issue no 19 which focused on the role of Yemeni women in peacebuilding.  
**Significance of the outcome**: This opportinaty demonstrated the CAP's experiences and capabilities in the field of women and peace, also visibility for women in  peacebuilding in Yemen, by participating in providing journalistic work and consulting women on topics, and this is new and good in expanding the activities of advocacy campaigns and adding work with the media in advocacy campaigns.
