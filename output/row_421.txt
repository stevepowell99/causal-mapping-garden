**Outcome description**: On 10th November 2023, the Secretary General of South Sudan Youth Constitutional Working Group, Mr. Saviour Tombe, was appointed a speaker of the East African Youth Parliament in Arusha, Tanzania. 
**Significance of the outcome**: Though this outcome is more beneficial to Mr. Saviour Tombe, it is equally significant to our project goal because it enables us to easily garner youth voices and support from the regional level.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: International community
**Scale**: Regional
**Contribution of the programme**: This outcome was partly a result of his active engagement in South Sudan’s constitution making process through our project activities such as radio talk shows among others because, Mr. Saviour is a member of the East African Youth Network, a space which could have played a greater role thereof. However, in his appreciation post on WhatsApp and Facebook, Mr. Saviour said that his role as Secretary General of South Sudan Youth Constitutional Working Group played a significant part in his appointment.
