**Outcome description**: In July 2023, the Dutch government was challenged in the Netherlands, on not condemning the US deliveries of cluster munitions to Ukraine. Parliamentary questions were asked by the SP on cluster munitions deliveries to Ukraine by the US, breaking the silence in parliament, and multiple media outlets reflected our views including an oped in a national newspaper and prime time Nieuwsuur coverage.  
**Significance of the outcome**: The Convention on Cluster Munitions is signed by the Netherlands, and includes the obligation to " promote the norms it establishes and shall make its best efforts to discourage States not party to this Convention from using cluster munitions. ". Strong implementation of the Convention on Cluster Munitions, and condemnation of use by anyone anywhere is important for the stigma on cluster munitions
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Civic change agents
**Scale**: National
**Contribution of the programme**: PAX is a long term former steering committee member of the Cluster Munition Coalition, striving for a world without cluster munitions. We did multiple media on cluster munitions deliveries to Ukraine too in 2023. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Yes, worldwide action was being taken by CMC members to condemn the US deliveries of cluster munitions to Ukraine.
