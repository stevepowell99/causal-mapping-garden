**Outcome description**: In 2023, there was an increase in support for youth activities on the constitution making process from government authorities entrusted to oversee this process in Juba. For exampe, during our face-to-face meeting with the Secretary General of Reconstituted National Constitutional Review Commission held in 15 April 2023 at his office, Honourable John Natana expressed great interest working with South Sudanese youth through the South Sudan Youth Constitutional Working Group throughout the constitution making process.
**Significance of the outcome**: This significant proclamation of support is a testament that we have a strong collaborator through whom we can amplify youth voices in the constitution making process.
