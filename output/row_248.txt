**Outcome description**: In September 2023, HRD (King Burundian) was forced into exlie following the Burundian Govenments' statement threatening bloggers over a statement he posted on human rights violations in Burundi. 
**Significance of the outcome**: In September 2023, a Burundian HRD and Blogger (King Burundian) posted a blog on human right violations by the Burundian government and security agencies. Following this, the Minister of Internal Affairs in Burundi issued threatening statements to Burundian bloggers including (@kingBurundian, now @king Umurundi Freedom) over the information he had published on human rights violations. The program supported the blogger to resettle into exile. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: Following the monitoring and documentation of human rights violations in Burundi, HRD and Blogger (King Burundian) published a blog exposing the Burundian government and security over human rights violations they had committed. On October 17, 2023, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Internal Affairs held  a press conference and condemned the blogger and accused them of s using pysuedonyms to send wrong information that divides the nation. The minister also mentioned that they had a list of all the real names of the bloggers. This made the blogger to flee to exile in Kenya and Later in Uganda. The program partner (CBDDH) was working with the bloggers (@kingBurundian, now @king Umurundi Freedom)  as a monitor, and sends data to the Coalition and also verify information which is used to produce reports and news letters.
