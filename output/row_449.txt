**Outcome description**: In September 2024, the Jie and Toposa attended peace concepts workshop together and celebrated the International day of Peace on the 21st September 2025 in Kuron.
**Significance of the outcome**: Prior to this, these communities were isolated from each other and no proximity due to the violent relationship existed between them. There has been no record of Toposa and Jie freely attending functions such as workshops and trainings including cultural celebrations in one place without the moderations from the peace department in Kuron. The participants of these workshops were invited when they were already living in Toposa land contrary to before when they were picked from Boma and then returned thereafter by the Peace Village vehicles. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: It is important to note that these Jie people were already living within the Toposa villages and they were mobilized to attend the workshop together and consequently celebrate the International Day of Peace in Kuron. 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Local
**Contribution of the programme**: The Peace Village conducted two dialogue meetings in June and July 2024 that mediated a violent conflict that killed one Toposa man. The conflict was solved through blood compensation. This opened a peaceful co-existence between the Toposa and the Jie community
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: no
**Rating contribution**: great
