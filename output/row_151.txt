**Outcome description**: In March 2022, one of the former students of AUNHOR established a civil society organization, 'Ahimsa' that  focuses on spreading the culture of non-violence within the youth community in the North West in Syria by launching trainings and activities for youths.  
**Significance of the outcome**: There is a lack of organizations that focus on the culture and methodology of non-violence among the youth-activists community in North Syria. Ahimsa is an added value to the community as there is a high need to counter the culture of violence in north Syria. 
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Regional
**Contribution of the programme**: The founder, Alva, has followed the courses in the university of non-violence AUNOHR which inspired her to take what she learned into her community by establishing Ahimsa. 
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: Yes- the founder is also one of the youths who followed trainings offered by Kulluna Muwatinum project. Her experience through this project and joining AUNOHR courses empowered her to take the step and establish 'Ahimsa'
