**Outcome description**: In May 2021, a gender based violence forum is promoting interaction amongst the women groups in Warrap and Northern Bhar el Gazal state. 
**Significance of the outcome**: This Gender Based Violence forum is the first of its kind in Warrap and Northern Bahr el Ghazal state. There was a gap between women at the grassroots and their colleagues at the state level. Previously there were not women traditional chiefs as well as knowing their roles. The community at grassroots were ignorant of the existence female chiefs.
