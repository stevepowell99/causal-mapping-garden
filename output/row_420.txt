**Outcome description**: On 10th November 2023, the chiefs and community members of Mukaya in Jezira and Atende Gimunu Boma, Yei River County of Central Eqautoria State, South Sudan, agreed and signed a peace charter to participate in a cultural land cleansing ceremony in Yei River County.
**Significance of the outcome**: Before the dialogue the different communities in the camps were not in harmony, due to suspission and mistust. The cultural land cleansing ceremony is to promote reconciliation and forgiveness for atrocities committed in Mukaya Payam by whoever was involved during the armed conflict. This has encouraged the Mukaya people in the Internally displaced camps and in the refugee camps in Uganda to freely return to Mukaya Payam.
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: medium
**Social actor**: Engaged citizens
**Scale**: Regional
**Contribution of the programme**: RECONCILE conducted two conflict transformation and peace building trainings between September and October 2023 for 89 participants selected from the two internally displaced camps in Yei (Atende and Jezira). Conducted one community dialogue in Gimunu Boma, Yei Town Payam, Yei River County from 8th -10th November 2023. This was a neutral place for the two camps. These activities have initiated the change.
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: This was a process that involved other actors from 2021 and the final dialogue (Community Progresss organisation (CEPO), the community leades were some of the actors involved)
**Rating contribution**: moderate
