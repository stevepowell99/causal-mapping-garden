**Outcome description**: In 2024, male-dominated media in the WANA region started to talk about forced displacement in Syria using feminist/gender sensitive language based on the book published by WND 
**Significance of the outcome**: This is an important change because it provided additional coverage to the plight and narrative of women who experienced forced displacement in Syria while the experience of women especially syrian women who have experienced various human rights violations have not been widely talked about in male dominated media in the WANA Region
**Additional information (use if needed) **: 
**Rating significance**: large
**Social actor**: Media
**Scale**: Organisation
**Contribution of the programme**: WND produced the book as part of its work under the programme and further presented the book at several event (during a launch event in Berlin in 2024 and invited to an event organized by Heinrich Boell Stiftung in Berlin in 2025) and sent the book to various personalities from the civil society which helped to disseminate the book including to media and journalists
**Contribution of other actors and factors**: 
**Rating contribution**: great
**The change was unintended**: NO
