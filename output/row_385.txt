**Outcome description**: In BF, in 2023, local people used the monitors at least 20 times as channels for denouncing human rights violations and abuses and for obtaining assistance
**Significance of the outcome**: Some local organisations did not have the skills to carry out MDR work in the field, nor were they well known in this field before. Folearly/smalling the capacity-building and retraining of monitors from local associations and the monitoring work carried out, the visibility of these local organisations has increased and the monitors are constantly approached by victims or their close relatives to report cases of abuse and/or violations and to receive assistance.
